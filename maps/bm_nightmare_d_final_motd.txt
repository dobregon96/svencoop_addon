---* Black Mesa Nightmare *---


Final Version

Visit the Nightmare site at http://www.volcom-mapping.com
Map is meant for 3-8 players
Gameplay lasts for about 1-2 hours


Sector D - Sewage System
0800 Hours
Black Mesa, New Mexico

Chapter 4: Surface Tension

You have reached the Black Mesa Waste Processing Compound, several miles above Sector D. All that remains is to
find a way off this facility... Somewhere in the far distance, the sound of rotors can be heard.. 

"You've made it..."


Details:

You must use teamwork to beat this map!
This comes in many forms from:

1) Ducking to boost another player to a high ledge
2) Holding a door open for another player
3) And many more!

Good luck!
