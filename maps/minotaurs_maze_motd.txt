The Minotaurs Maze 
By Butt Ugly Mutha Fucka

The goverment has discovered an underground maze thought to 
be home to the legendary minotaur of Greek mythology.  The 4 Squads 
sent down to investigate have not returned.  Its now your turn
to check out the maze and slay the minotaur. WARNING-The military squads
orders are to kill any non military personel, they have no idea your 
trying to help. Proceed with caution...
