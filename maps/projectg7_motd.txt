============PROJECT: GUILTY - Mission 1===================

Mission Info: 

A large South American temple has been taken over by a Columbian
drug lord and his army, who wishes to use the treasures in the 
temple to fund his anti-US activities

We have recieved word that a document proving a link between the
Columbian Government and this Drug Lord can be found deep within 
the temple where the troops base is located. Your mission is to
retrieve this document

There have been some strange reports of mutated creatures throughout
the facility, possibly the result of genetic experiments. Be careful
