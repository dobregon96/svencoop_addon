Sector D - Sewage System
0650 Hours
Black Mesa, New Mexico

Chapter 3: Ascension

You have entered the Sector D sewage system. There are rumors that there is a Sector D 
elevator leading to the surface, however, finding that may not be easy...

"The surface awaits..."


Details:

You must use teamwork to beat this map!
This comes in many forms from:

1) Ducking to boost another player to a high ledge
2) Holding a door open for another player
3) And many more!

Good luck!
